**I built an AI interview coach because I was tired of going into multi-round interviews blind.**

Here's the problem: you get the recruiter email, a JD link, maybe the names of your interviewers. Then you're on your own.

So I built InterviewPrepAI -- an AI agent that:

- Analyzes the JD, company, and stage to generate tailored questions (not generic "tell me about yourself" lists)
- Scores your answers across role-specific competencies in real-time (technical, sales acumen, communication, etc.)
- Runs full mock interviews with a realistic interviewer persona -- and gives you feedback after every answer
- Shows you where you match the JD and where the gaps are

The stack: React + FastAPI + LangGraph + Supabase + GPT-4o-mini. The whole thing runs as a state machine -- parse, analyze, generate, roleplay, evaluate, summarize -- with persistent sessions so you can come back and drill again.

It's live now. First session is free.
